# Exopilot Product Requirement Document (PRD)

## 1. Product Vision & Background
Indonesian agricultural Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) exporting high-value spices (Nutmeg, Cloves, Mace, Cinnamon, Cocoa, Coffee) currently manage operations across fragmented, non-collaborative channels: WhatsApp, personal Excel sheets, paper logs, and unorganized email threads. This creates high operational risks, including:
- Missed buyer follow-ups (forgotten chat threads)
- Ocean freight document misplacement (missing BL, invoice mismatch)
- Inability to quickly calculate cargo limits or multi-currency margin impacts
- Dynamic market price fluctuations leading to sub-optimal sales negotiations

**Exopilot** is a unified "Export Office Operating System" that consolidates buyer pipeline CRM, live container logs, supplier scoring networks, market charts, and automated trade documents into a unified, high-fidelity experience.

## 2. Core Features (The 9 Modules)
1. **Interactive Dashboard Command Center**: Bento-style summary cards of active pipelines, vessel transits, total valuations, active supplier counts, and dynamic agricultural commodity indexes.
2. **Buyer Inquiry CRM Pipeline**: Comprehensive registry of incoming buyer bids, source channels (Alibaba, LinkedIn, Direct), target prices, volume requirements, follow-up calendar alerts, and status selectors.
3. **Buyer Lifetime CRM Profile**: Tracking historical customer deal records, lifetime export weight volumes, preferred spices, bank transaction reliability ratings, and trust tiers.
4. **Vessel Cargo Shipment Tracker**: International cargo container registry logs, carrier details, ETD origin and ETA destination checklists, and clickable document uploads (BL, invoice, packing list).
5. **Farmer Supplier Management Network**: Local farmer co-operative registers, geographical sourcing origins, monthly supply limits, historical unit rates, stars rating reliability metrics, and legal compliance trackers.
6. **Agricultural Market Exchange**: Live index tickers of spice rates per Metric Ton (MT), HSL-styled 30-day SVG sparkline historical charts, and 7-day profit gains.
7. **Negotiation Tactical Notes**: Deal logs categorized by Preference, Risk, and Payments, equipped with search and key-tag filters.
8. **AI Document Studio**: Automated trade generator producing formal Quotations, Invoices, and Contracts addressing customer constraints, integrated with a high-fidelity stationary printable paper preview and jsPDF exporter.
9. **Account Profile & Settings**: SME business settings, agricultural license numbers (NIB), and contact registries.

## 3. High Fidelity Tools
- **Container Capacity Calculator**: Instant pack capacity estimator for 20ft GP, 40ft GP, and 40ft HQ ocean containers using custom dimensions (length, width, height, weight) with quick packaging presets.
- **Dynamic Multi-Currency Margin Converter**: Real-time cross-currency converter and margin simulator showing margin adjustments, total product cost, sales revenue, and profit values in USD and Indonesian Rupiah (IDR).
